Instructions

  1. Download the app from the App Store onto your mobile phone.
  2. Once you are on OneLink's homepage, choose the sign-in option and admin type that fits your criteria.
  3. Enter your username, password, and school name to access the app's features.
  4. Navigate through clubs, the calendar, the camera, the attendance, and the grade book to better understand your child's school life.

It's as easy as creating your login and gaining access to your 5 features that help guide you through primary education. OneLink's goal is to create an inviting atmosphere where parents, students, and teachers can all stay connected.
